CRAP Website Evaluations

Apple

  • Contrast: Strong contrast through clean typography, clear headings, and controlled color usage, making key content easy to find and read.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, spacing, button styles, and layout patterns across pages create a unified look and strong brand identity.
  • Alignment: Content follows a clear grid and lines up consistently, making the page feel organized and professional.
  • Proximity: Related elements (product info, pricing, calls-to-action) are grouped together, making the page easy to scan and understand.
