CRAP Website Evaluations

Apple

  • Contrast: Strong contrast through clean typography, clear headings, and controlled color usage, making key content easy to find and read.
  • Repetition: Consistent fonts, spacing, button styles, and layout patterns across pages create a unified look and strong brand identity.
  • Alignment: Content follows a clear grid and lines up consistently, making the page feel organized and professional.
  • Proximity: Related elements (product info, pricing, calls-to-action) are grouped together, making the page easy to scan and understand.

Yale School of Art

  • Contrast: The contrast on the site is not very good because there is a lot of text and content on the page, but nothing really stands out. It can be hard to tell what information is most important.
  • Repetition: There is very little repetition in the design. Fonts, layouts, and colors change between pages, which makes the site feel inconsistent and unorganized.
  • Alignment: The alignment is weak since many elements do not line up cleanly. The page feels cluttered and is difficult to read or scan quickly.
  • Proximity: Proximity is poor because related information is not always grouped together, while unrelated content is placed close to each other, making the page confusing to navigate.
