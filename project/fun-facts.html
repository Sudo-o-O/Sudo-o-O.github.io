Fun Watermelon Facts

Watermelons are more interesting than you might think! Here are some fun and surprising facts about this popular fruit.

Fact #1

Watermelons are about 90% water, making them extremely refreshing.

Fact #2

Watermelons were first grown in Africa over 5,000 years ago.

Fact #3

The largest watermelon ever recorded weighed over 350 pounds.

Fact #4

Watermelons can be red, yellow, orange, and even white inside.

Fact #5

Watermelon is technically both a fruit and a vegetable.

Fact #6

Every part of the watermelon is edible, including the rind.
